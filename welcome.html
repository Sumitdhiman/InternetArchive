How to use

1
Keyboard shortcut — Press Ctrl + Shift + F on any web page to instantly open its archived version.
2
Extension icon — Click the toolbar icon to preview the archive URL and open it with one click.
3
Right-click menu — Right-click any page, link, image, or video and choose "Open in Internet Archive".

This extension is free and open-source. If it saves you time,
